How to Use Note-AI

1

Start Recording: Click the "Start Recording" button or press Alt + N to begin.

2

Speak Your Note: Say "note" followed by your thoughts. For example, "note, I have a meeting on Monday."

3

Stop Recording: Click the "Stop Recording" button or press Alt + S to stop.

4

Add Notes Manually: Type your note in the textarea below and click "Add Note."

5

Edit or Delete Notes: Use the "Edit" and "Delete" buttons on each note to manage your notes.

6

Toggle Dark/Light Mode: Use the toggle switch in the top-right corner to switch between themes.
